Your Portfolio Is a Bank. Start Using It Like One.

There’s a way to borrow against your portfolio at near-Treasury rates. No bank. No credit check. No selling a single position.

Box Spreads · May 5, 2026

AG

Akiva Glazerson

Principal, STQ Capital

 4 min read

Most people assume that when they need cash, they have two options: sell something or go to a bank. There’s a third option – and it’s better than both. A box spread is a borrowing structure that lets you take a loan against your investment portfolio at near-Treasury rates. No application. No credit check. No selling appreciated positions. The rate is set by the market – not a broker, not a bank – and it’s typically well below what any bank or wirehouse will offer you. The interest is potentially tax-deductible. You stay fully invested the entire time.
