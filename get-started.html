Let's Get You Set Up.

Opening an account with STQ takes about 10 minutes. Here's exactly what to expect and what you'll need.

The Process

Simple. Secure. Done in one sitting.

We've designed onboarding to be as straightforward as possible. You submit your information once, securely, and we handle the rest. No back-and-forth, no paperwork maze.

01

Submit your information.

Click the button, complete the secure form, and upload any documents. Takes about 10 minutes. You can stop and return at any time.

02

We review and prepare your account.

We review your submission and prepare your Schwab account. You'll hear back within one business day with next steps and any follow-up questions.

03

Sign and fund.

You'll receive a DocuSign envelope with your account opening documents. Sign electronically, fund your account via bank transfer or ACAT, and you're live.

04

We get to work.

Once funded, we build your portfolio based on your tax situation, objectives, and the strategy you've discussed. You'll receive a full portfolio summary before any trades are placed.

What to Have Ready.
Gather these before you start — it'll take less than 10 minutes if you have them on hand.
Government-issued ID
Driver's license or passport — a photo is fine
Social Security Number
Required for account opening
Employment information
Employer name, occupation, annual income range
Beneficiary details
Name, relationship, date of birth, SSN if available
Bank account for funding
Routing and account number for initial transfer
Most recent account statement
If transferring from another broker — one statement is enough
Don't have everything? That's fine. Submit what you have and we'll follow up on anything missing.
